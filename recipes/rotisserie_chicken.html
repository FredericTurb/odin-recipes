Rotisserie Chicken

Description of the dish:

A rotisserie chicken is a whole chicken that has been seasoned and cooked on a rotating spit, usually over an open flame or in an oven.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 whole chicken (1.5 to 2kg).
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil.
  3. 2 teaspoons salt.
  4. 1 teaspoon black pepper
  5. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. 1 teaspoon onion powder
  7. 1 teaspoon paprika
  8. 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or your choice of herbs)
  9. 1 lemon, halved
  10. Fresh Herbs (like rosemary or thyme) for stuffing (optional)

  • Roast the Chicken: Roast in the preheated oven for about 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C) in the thickest part of the thigh.
  • Rest the Chicken: Rest for about 10-15 minutes before carving. This allows the juices to redistribute.
